Our Story

Founded in 1990, our factory has grown from a small workshop to one of the leading footwear manufacturers in the region. We specialize in producing high-quality shoes for various market segments, from casual wear to formal and sports footwear.

Our success is built on a foundation of skilled craftsmanship, cutting-edge technology, and an unwavering commitment to quality. We work with premium materials and employ rigorous quality control measures to ensure every pair of shoes meets the highest standards.

Today, we serve clients across multiple continents, producing millions of pairs annually while maintaining our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction.
